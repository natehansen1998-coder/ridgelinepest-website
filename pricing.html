Common Questions

Do I have to sign a contract?

No. There are no contracts and no cancellation fees. You can stop your service at any time with no penalty. We keep customers by doing good work — not by trapping them in agreements.

What if pests come back between visits?

We come back for free. All recurring plans include free re-service if pests return between your scheduled treatments. Just give us a call.

What pests are covered?

All plans cover general pest control, spiders, and wasps/bees. This includes ants, earwigs, centipedes, silverfish, black widows, hobo spiders, and most common Utah County pests. Call us if you have a specific pest question.

Are your products safe for kids and pets?

Yes. We use eco-friendly products that are safe for your family and pets once dry. We'll let you know how long to stay off treated areas after each visit.

How do I get started?
