Common Household Pests We Treat

Utah County homes face unique pest challenges throughout the year. Our technicians are experts at identifying and eliminating all types of household pests:

  • Ants - Including carpenter ants, pavement ants, and sugar ants
  • Spiders - From common house spiders to black widows and hobo spiders
  • Rodents - Mice and rats that can damage your home and spread disease
  • Wasps & Bees - Safe removal of stinging insects from your property
  • Cockroaches - Complete elimination of these resilient pests
  • Earwigs & Silverfish - Moisture-loving pests common in Utah basements
  • Box Elder Bugs - Seasonal invaders that swarm Utah homes

Seasonal Pest Prevention

In Utah County, pest activity changes with the seasons. Our year-round protection plans keep your home defended against seasonal invaders:

  • Spring - Ants and spiders emerge as temperatures warm
  • Summer - Wasps, mosquitoes, and outdoor pests peak
  • Fall - Box elder bugs and rodents seek indoor shelter
  • Winter - Mice and rats become the primary concern
